Grex is located in Ann Arbor, Michigan, a city of about 110,000 people in the northern midwest part of the United States. Ann Arbor is best known as the home of the University of Michigan, a public university with more than 30,000 students. The city was founded in 1824, and is named for the wives of its two founders (both named Ann), and for its natural groves, or arbors.
