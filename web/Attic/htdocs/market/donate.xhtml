Donate to Grex

Grex is a service offered by Cyberspace Communications, Inc. In spite of the corporate sounding name, Cyberspace is a non-profit organization, staffed entirely by volunteers and doing nothing except running Grex. It is funded almost entirely by various kinds of donations from our users. Since we are a registered 501(c)3 corporation, all donations are tax-deductable.

If you want to help support Grex, you can become a member, or you can just send us some money.

You can make donations by mail by sending a check to:

Cyberspace Communications, Inc.
P.O. Box 4432
Ann Arbor, MI 48106-4432 (USA)

If you'd like to make a donation by credit card, you can do so via Paypal. Click on the icon below, and enter the amount you'd like to donate on the form that follows. Any special instructions on how your donation is to be used may be included in the comments box.

If you have questions, please contact the Grex treasurer at treasurer@grex.org.
