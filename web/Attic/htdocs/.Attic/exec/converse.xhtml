Conversing with other users on Grex

There are basically three ways to communicate with other users. Conferencing, which is public, is one. E-mail, which is private, is a second. The third method is Real-Time communication. This requires that the other people must be logged in at the same time.

There are several approaches to real-time communications: They include write, ntalk, and party.

There are three ways to find out who is logged onto Grex at the moment. They are w, who and finger. Each one offers a slightly different amount of data in a slightly different format.
