On-line Games on Grex

  • adventure, Colossal Cave Adventure, the first text adventure game.
  • arithmetic, a game of solving equations. Ctrl-C to end.
  • backgammon, the board game. Look out for the doubling cube!
  • banner, creates a banner to print out on your printer.
  • battlestar, an interactive science fiction text adventure.
  • bj, the classic game of Black Jack. Ctrl-C to end.
  • boggle, the word search game.
  • canfield, a solitaire card game.
  • ching, the Chinese form of divination. Enter your question, then hit Ctrl-D.
  • craps, the dice game. Ctrl-C to end.
  • cribbage, the card game.
  • factor, finds the factors of any number you enter. Ctrl-D to end.
  • fish, the card game of Go Fish.
  • figlet, the program to print your text in large ASCII fonts. Ctrl-D to quit.
  • figlist, lists every figlet font available on Grex.
  • fortune, a message from a cookie.
  • hack, a dungeon adventure game.
  • hangman, the word game.
  • mathom, the virtual gift-giving program.
  • mille, the card game Mille Bournes.
  • monop, the game of Monopoly.
  • nethack, a dungeon adventure game.
  • rain, which draws raindrops on your screen. Ctrl-C to end.
  • robots, avoid the robots as long as you can. If you've played Daleks, this is the same thing.
  • snake, avoid the snake while grabbing money. H=left, J=down, K=up, L=right, Ctrl-C to end.
  • trek, the classic computer game. Move around the galaxy destroying Klingons.
  • worm, move around the screen eating money. H=left, J=down, K=up, L=right, Ctrl-C to end.
  • worms, watch the worms crawl around your screen.
  • wump, Hunt the Wumpus. Search for the wumpus in a network of caves, avoiding obstacles. earch
