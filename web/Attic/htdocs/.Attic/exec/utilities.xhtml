Handy little utilitiy programs on Grex

There are many little programs written to do useful things. Here is a partial list of the utilities that you can run.
  • filebrowse is a utility you can use to list your files, read them, or delete them.
  • The change program will change many of your settings, including your password and your pager (See note below). Remember, some of these changes won't take effect until the next time you log in.
  • Grex has several different text editors to choose from.
  • A graphic view of Grex terminal usage.
  • How much Disk Space is free?
  • How much Disk Space am I using?
  • View the archived minutes from past meetings of the Grex Board.
Note: A pager is a little program that stops runaway output at each page and gives you a chance to read it before it sends any more. filebrowse is a good example of this.
