Grex is constantly evolving, and to get a sense of what's changing, you've really got to login to check it out. In particular, Grex went through a period of neglect for several years, where it was barely functioning and most of the original staff members left. The user base became largely apathetic,

Since then, however, Grex has undergone something of a renaissance, and is once again flourishing.

As always, we depend on volunteers to keep Grex up to date and working correctly. If you have an interest in helping Grex in any way, please e-mail the staff help@grex.org and let them know you'd like to help.
