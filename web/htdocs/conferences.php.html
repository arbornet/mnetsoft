Access M-Net Conferences

Particpating in conferences on M-Net is one of the primary reason why people why many people use M-Net on a daily or even hourly basis.

You can access M-Net's 100+ conferences by telnet/ssh, or via the Web.

In order to access M-Net's conferences via the web, you must already have an account. For more information about how to set up an account, click here.

When you click on the link below, you will be prompted for your m-net user ID (login) and password.
