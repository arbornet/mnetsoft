The PicoSpan Conferencing System

PicoSpan conferencing permits conversations to proceed among the many users of Grex. If you have never used the system before, read the PicoSpan Guide first. There are many conferences on Grex, and just four of them are listed below. Picking any of the these links will take you into PicoSpan and the specified conference. Type q or quit to return to lynx.
  • Agora is the main Grex conference. It is the only one that is restarted regularly 4 times a year.
  • Info is a conference containing lots of information about how to use the system. You can also get help here.
  • Coop is where we discuss Grex and make plans for its future. It is open to members and non-members alike.
  • Ann Arbor/Ypsi is a conference for discussing matters in the local area of Ann Arbor, Michigan. Anyone may join, though.
There are many other conferences and more advanced features. If you fair-witness a conference, you might want to look at popcorn's f-w cheat sheet to find out what you can do.
