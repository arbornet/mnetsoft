Party

"Party" is a program that lets you exchange one-line messages with a group of people. Anyone logged into Grex may use party. From here, you may see who is using party at the moment.

Select here to enter party yourself. While you're in "party", type h to get help, or type a response for others to see. Before you enter a response, you must hit the space bar so the party program knows you're not entering a command for it.

There are also other things you can do involving the party program.
