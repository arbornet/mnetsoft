Limitations for email

Because the Grex system is paid for by donations, we are unable to aford an expensive high-speed internet connection. On our low-speed connection, problems arise unless users are good citizens. Therefore, the hard-working staff of Grex request that you limit your use of email in the following ways.
  • Please limit the length of incoming and outgoing messages to 100k.
  • Please do not store mail in your inbox for long periods of time.
  • Please do not set up mail aliases of large mailing lists.
  • If you keep sent or received mail in folders, please download those folders to your home computer on a regular basis. Disk space is at a premium.
