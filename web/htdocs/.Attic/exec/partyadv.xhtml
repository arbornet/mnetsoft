Other Things You Can Do With Party

  • Enter a different channel, besides the default channel "party".
  • Get a listing of party logs which you can read.
  • Search a party log for a word, name, or other string.
  • Read a party log in real time, without having to join that channel. (Temporarily disabled.)
  • Read the last ten lines of a party log.
  • Read the Party Frequently Asked Questions List.
  • Read the man page for party.
Please note: Channels whose names end in "_x" are private channels. You may try to join them, however, the people in the party may or may not elect to let you join.
