Trouble With Grex

If you are having trouble with Grex, and you need help, then this page is the right one for you to look at. Here you will find pointers to places where you may be able to get your question answered. If you cannot figure out the solution based on the information here, then you can learn how to get sufficient information about your problem to report it to the Grex Staff.

First you have to select what kind of trouble you are having with Grex. Please choose from the following menu:

  • You cannot get SSH or telnet to connect to Grex.
  • You can get SSH or telnet to connect to Grex, but you cannot log in.
  • You can log in, but you do not get the right prompt.
  • You can log in, but you can't get mail to work.
  • You have some other problem, or you just want to ask a question about something.
