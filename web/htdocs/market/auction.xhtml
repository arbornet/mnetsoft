The Great Grex Auction!

Thank you for being the high bidder on an item in the Grex auction. As soon as Grex receives payment for the item, the auctioneers will authorize the donor to deliver it to you. There are a couple of ways to send auction payments to Grex:

  • Send a check or money order to:
    Cyberspace Communications, Inc.
    P.O. Box 4432
    Ann Arbor, MI 48106-4432 (USA)
  • Send money with your credit card, via Paypal. To do that, click on the icon below, and enter the amount you'd like to donate on the form that follows. It would help if you also sent a separate mail to auction@grex.org to let us know which items you just paid for. (But if you forget, we can probably figure it out.)

If you have questions, please contact the Grex auctioneers at auction@grex.org.

Thanks again for supporting Grex!
